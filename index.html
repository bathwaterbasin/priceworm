Rules of Engagement

01. Wait for the Wormhole
Monitor each scheduled wormhole time. Observe the pivots that form during these sacred moments.
02. Seek New Highs and Lows
After a wormhole, wait for price to form a new high or new low that gets retested during the next session.
03. The Retest is Sacred
The retest of the new high/low is your entry signal. This is where the worm emerges from the depths.
04. Honor the Last Low
Set your stop loss at the last significant low or near it. The earth beneath must hold.
05. Exit at the Horizon
Take profits at session close or the next pivot/wormhole. Sometimes the worm surfaces, sometimes it dives deeper.
06. The Safest Path
You may trade immediately at wormhole time, but it is always safest to wait until the next session open to decide direction.
07. Continuation Protocol
If the next wormhole shows continuation signals, margin may be added and stop loss moved to breakeven for protection against reversal.

Risk Doctrine

⚠ CAPITAL PRESERVATION IS PARAMOUNT ⚠

R2. Position sizing must align with the probability of the unknown working against us
R3. When in doubt, reduce position size or step aside entirely
R4. Keep detailed records of what worked, what didn't, and what we learned
